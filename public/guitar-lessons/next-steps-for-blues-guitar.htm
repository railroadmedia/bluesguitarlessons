Welcome to the final blues guitar lesson. Congratulations on making it through the entire series! At this point, you’re probably wondering what comes next for you. It can be hard to know where to go next and how to bring yourself up to the next level.

The most important thing you can do is set small, attainable goals. Doing this makes progress much more measurable and you’ll be far more motivated to keep picking up your guitar and getting better.

If you really want to get further into the blues, it’s worth it to take the time to learn a few of your favorite blues tunes. You could also learn the rest of the blues scale shapes. This will help build up your techniques and blues guitar vocabulary.
